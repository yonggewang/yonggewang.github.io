Total amount of tokens

There will be a total of 1 billion (1,000,000,000) tokens issued (the schedule for issuing these tokens will be release, normally should be issued within 5 years). From the second years on, the governance mechanisms may decide to issue at most 2% CSWP tokens annually in future. The first 1 billion CSWP tokens will be allocated as follows
  1. 80% will be reserved for liquidity providers. It will be released from April 2021 to March 2022
  2. 10% is reserved for the development team. It will be released during April 2022 to March 2023
  3. 3% for marketing purpose. This will be released based on the demands of needs after June 2021
  4. 7% is reserved for early investment organizations
